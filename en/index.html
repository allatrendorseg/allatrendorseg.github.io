About

People for Animals 2013 Foundation

Our foundation is an organization of citizen volunteers that was founded in 2013. Our founding members have several years of animal rescue experience. For us, animal rescue is a vocation, and we do it voluntarily in our spare time.

Our foundation aims to help animals’ well-being and quality of life and to immediately rescue animals that have fallen victim to animal cruelty by cooperating with the authorities.
